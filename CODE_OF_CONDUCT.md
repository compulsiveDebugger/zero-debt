# Code of Conduct

Adopted from the [Contributor Covenant 2.1](https://www.contributor-covenant.org/version/2/1/code_of_conduct/). Lightly adapted for an internal corporate tool.

## Our Pledge

We pledge to make participation in zero-debt a harassment-free experience for everyone.

## Our Standards

**Encouraged behavior:**
- Be specific and direct in technical feedback.
- Distinguish the idea from the person.
- Acknowledge contributions; credit work.
- Assume good intent; ask before escalating.

**Unacceptable behavior:**
- Personal attacks, insults, or derogatory comments.
- Harassment of any kind, public or private.
- Publishing others' private information without permission.
- Sustained disruption of discussions or reviews.
- Sexualized language or imagery.

## Scope

Applies in all project spaces — repository, PR comments, issues, internal chat channels tagged for zero-debt — and when an individual is officially representing the project.

## Enforcement

Report violations to the project maintainers via the security/conduct contact in [SECURITY.md](SECURITY.md). Reports are confidential.

Maintainers may take any action they deem appropriate, including:

1. **Correction** — private written feedback.
2. **Warning** — formal warning with stated consequences for continued behavior.
3. **Temporary ban** — from project spaces for a stated period.
4. **Permanent ban** — from all project spaces.
