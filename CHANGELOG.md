`Added`, `Changed`, `Deprecated`, `Removed`, `Fixed`, `Security`.

When you ship a change, add an entry under `## Unreleased` in the same PR. Release tooling (see [docs/RELEASING.md](docs/RELEASING.md), added in M9) promotes `Unreleased` to a versioned section at tag time.
